About Fresh Water Initiative

We are a non-profit organization which raises awereness about communities with limited clean water.




Mission & Vision

Our mission and vision is to provide support for these communities by raising funds to help those in need.




Goal

Our goal is to raise awereness across South Africa and the world, about the hardships some people face every day with regards to clean water, which is a basic human right.


We strongly believe that, if we all stand together to help those in need we can, we can keep South Africa and the world hydrated
